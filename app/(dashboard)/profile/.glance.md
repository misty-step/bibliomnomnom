### Technical Gotchas

- **Client-Side Execution**: The use of the `"use client"` directive at the route level means that all components within this tree, including the error and suspense boundaries, are initialized on the client.
- **Suspense Integration**: The presence of `Suspense` implies that the underlying `ProfilePage` component utilizes concurrent features or data-fetching hooks that trigger a suspension of the component tree while waiting for data.
- **Error Handling**: The `ErrorBoundary` is configured with a specific `ErrorState` fallback, ensuring that any failures in the profile's data fetching or rendering do not crash the entire dashboard layout.
