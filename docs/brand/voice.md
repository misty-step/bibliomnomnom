# Bibliomnomnom Brand Voice

## Who We're Talking To

**Avid readers** who:
- Read 20+ books per year
- Care about *remembering* what they read
- Value privacy and data ownership
- Want a calm, thoughtful digital space
- Appreciate quality over quantity

## Voice Attributes

### Warm
We're a friendly companion on your reading journey, not a cold productivity tool.

**Do:** "Welcome back to your reading nook"
**Don't:** "User dashboard loaded"

### Intellectual
We respect our readers' intelligence without being pretentious.

**Do:** "Capture the insights that matter"
**Don't:** "Leverage synergistic knowledge retention paradigms"

### Privacy-Focused
Privacy is a feature, not an afterthought. We mention it naturally.

**Do:** "Your reading history stays yours"
**Don't:** "We will never sell your data to third parties pursuant to..."

### Quietly Confident
We know we're good. We don't need to shout.

**Do:** "The simplest way to remember what you read"
**Don't:** "THE BEST BOOK TRACKING APP EVER!!!"

## Tone Spectrum

| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Marketing | Warm, inviting, slightly whimsical |
| Onboarding | Encouraging, helpful |
| Empty states | Gentle, motivating |
| Errors | Apologetic, solution-focused |
| Success | Celebratory but not over-the-top |

## Key Messages

### Primary Value Proposition
> **"Your digital reading garden"**
> Track what you read, capture your thoughts, never forget a book.

### Privacy Angle
> **"Your reading life is yours"**
> No tracking. No ads. Just you and your books.

### Retention Focus
> **"Remember what you read"**
> Because the best books deserve to stay with you.

## Words We Use

| Instead of... | We say... |
|---------------|-----------|
| User | Reader |
| Dashboard | Library |
| Data | Reading history |
| Notes | Thoughts, reflections |
| Track | Remember, capture |
| Content | Books, reading |
| Features | Tools |

## Words We Avoid

- Synergy, leverage, optimize
- Hustle, grind, productivity hack
- Disrupting, revolutionizing
- Best-in-class, world-class
- Utilize (just say "use")
- Going forward (just say "from now on")

## Brand Personality

If Bibliomnomnom were a person, they'd be:
- A cozy bookshop owner who remembers your favorites
- Someone who values quality time with a good book
- Privacy-conscious but not paranoid
- Quietly competent, never boastful
- Warm but not saccharine

## The Name

**Bibliomnomnom** = Biblio (book) + om nom nom (the sound of happily consuming)

It's playful, memorable, and captures the joy of devouring books. Lean into the whimsy when appropriate, but don't overdo it.
